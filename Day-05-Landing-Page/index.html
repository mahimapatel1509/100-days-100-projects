With deep roots in Italy’s hospitality scene, our hospitality began in the year 2000 with the establishment of Silver Oak Hotel, a trusted name locally. Expanding our offerings, we introduced Shubh Kaaraj Marriage Garden & Silver Oak Vatika in 2008, quickly becoming a favoured venue for weddings and events. In 2016, we proudly launched Solaris Hotel—a 4-star property in the heart of Indore adjacent to Pipliyapala Lake and regional park Indore—dedicated to delivering excellence and embodying true hospitality. As a locally founded establishment, we bring the warmth and charm to every guest experience.

Our philosophy

From the Perspective of a couple

You do not come here to be entertained. You come here to experience something together. Morning espresso in the garden, sunlight filtering through the leaves, a midday visit to the island. Dinner in a small restaurant overlooking the water.

Practically speaking, everything is conveniently accessible: Milan is approximately one hour away. The Swiss border is only a short drive. Travel is comfortable - yet the atmosphere feels worlds apart. What remains is not an event - it is a feeling.

A place where conversation deepens. Where closeness feels natural. Where magic is not staged - it simply happens.

Calm, Cosy, Elegant

The white facade, the sheltered garden with its classical fountain and the forged round staircase is like the heart of the entire space twisting into a golden ratio.


Everything feels understated, almost effortless.


Inside, there is light, clean lines, natural material.


No exaggerated luxury - just space that allows you to breathe.
